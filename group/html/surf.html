Our Photographer

We have a photographer who has been freelancing for over 10 years and specialises in surf photography. Our photographer is there to capture your whole experience at Unstad Arctic Surf. Take home life long memories of you surfing in-between mountains, above the arctic circle, in the most beautiful place in the world!

Lessons

Learn the basic surfing fundamentals in a step-by-step surf lesson taught by certified instructors at Unstad Arctic Surf. Unstad is the perfect backdrop to learn with friendly waves, whilst the more advanced surfers can enjoy world class waves on the surf breaks further out.

We pride ourselves on our surf lessons and are teaching methods guarantee success!
