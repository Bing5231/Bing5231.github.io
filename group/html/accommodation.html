About

Welcome to Thon Hotel Svolvær
– a brand new and modern hotel at the seaside in Svolvær. Thon Hotel Svolvær have large and comfortable rooms with a view, restaurant, bar, gym, sauna (autumn 2022), large, modern conference facilities and its own fish farm. Centrally located in Svolvær, everything is ready for a great stay be it for work or leisure.

About

A destination hotel in beautiful surroundings for active people who want to enjoy a good night’s sleep in a comfortable bed. Scandic Vestfjord Lofoten is located on the quay in the center of Svolvær, with mountains and the sea as its closest neighbors. Our hotel has 63 rooms, each with their own identity and charm. We have a gym with treadmills and free weights. Our breakfast is served in our dining room which can accommodate over 100 people. In our lobby, we have several comfortable seating areas where our guests can meet or just relax with a good book.
