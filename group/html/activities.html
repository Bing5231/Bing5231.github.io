Activities

Kayak

By departing from the west cost of Lofoten, paddlers have the chance to experinece the best viewing location for sunsets and wildlife.

Surf Lesson

Take home a photo of you surfing in-between mountains, above the arctic circle, in the most beautiful place in the world!

Snorkeling

Snorkeling within the Arctic Circle with Lofoten Diving – PADI 5 Star Diving Resort.

Attractions

In Lofoten you will find several different nature attractions, galleries and museums. You can visit the Lofotr Viking Museum, the Lofoten Aquarium or stop at one of the many sculptures of Skulpturlandskap Nordland.
